Business Problem
The company lacks clear visibility into revenue, profit, and product performance across regions and time. Decision-makers need a dashboard to track performance and identify growth and opportunities.

Tools Used
Excel
Data Cleaning
Data Modelling
DAX

Key Insights
Revenue is concentrated in a few key regions.
A small group of products generates the majority of profit.
Some categories generate high revenue but low profit margins.
Monthly sales show seasonal fluctuations
Certain regions underperform consistently.

Business Recommendations
Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
Promote top-profit products more aggressively
Review pricing or cost structure for low-margin categories.
Investigate underperforming regions for operational issues.
Plan inventory based on seasonal sales trends.
