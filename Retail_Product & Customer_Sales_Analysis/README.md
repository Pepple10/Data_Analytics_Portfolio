**Retail Product \& Customer Sales Analysis**



**Business Problems**

The retail business wants to understand which products, categories and customers drive the most revenue.

The goal is to identify sales trends and improve product and marketing decisions.



**Tools Used**

* Excel (data cleaning, light calculations)
* MySQL (data cleaning, joins, analysis)
* Power BI (data visualization and dashboard)



**Key Insights**

* A small number of products generate the majority of revenue.
* Some product categories outperform others in total sales.
* A few customers contribute significantly to overall revenue.
* Sales show clear variation across time periods.



**Business Recommendations**

* Focus marketing on top-selling products.
* Increase inventory for high-performing categories.
* Offer loyalty incentives to high-value customer.
* Investigate low-performing products and adjust pricing or promotions.
