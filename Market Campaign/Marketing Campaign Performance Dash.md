**Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard**



**Overview**



This dashboard analyzes marketing campaign performance across multiple channels.

It focuses on revenue, engagement, and conversion effectiveness.



**Objectives**

* Track total revenue generated from campaigns
* Measure conversion performance by channel
* Analyze customer engagement (clicks vs impressions)
* Identify trends over time



**Key Metrics**

* Total Revenue
* Conversion Rate
* Total Clicks
* Total Impressions



**Dashboard Features**

* KPI cards with visual indicators (icons)
* Revenue breakdown by campaign
* Conversion rate by channel
* Engagement analysis using scatter plot
* Time-based revenue trend



**Insights**

* Certain campaigns generate higher revenue consistently
* Some channels drive high impressions but low conversions
* Conversion efficiency varies significantly across channels
* Revenue trends show periods of strong campaign performance



**Recommendations**

* Increase budget on high-converting campaigns
* Optimize low-performing channels
* Focus on improving conversion rates rather than just impressions
* Run A/B tests on underperforming campaigns
