Sales Performance Dashboard

Business Problem
The company ran multiple marketing campaigns but lacks clarity on which campaigns drive conversions and revenue. They need data analysis and visualization to optimize marketing spend and improve ROI.

Tools Used
Excel/CSV dataset
Python(Pandas, Matplotlib/Seaborn)
Power BI
Power Query
DAX

Key Insights

A small number of campaigns generated the highest conversion rates.
Customer response varied by age group and income level
Some campaigns had high impressions but low ROI
Conversion rate was a stronger performance indicator than total reach.

Business Recommendations
Increase budget allocation to high-converting campaigns.
Target customer segments with historically strong response rates
Discontinue or redesign low-ROI campaigns.
Track conversion rate and cost-per-conversion as primary KPIs.
